The Taiga Biome


    The taiga biome has a lot of pine trees that help supply a lot of the worlds oxygen. How does it supply so much? Because it has over 1/3 of the entire worlds trees! Because of all the healthy air, some animals live really well here, even during cold and snowy times!

    Some animals that live here are moose, wolves, bears, and squirrels. These animals have found ways to keep themselves from being pricked by all those pine needles.

    The temperature here is relatively cool and the weather is calm, but every once in a while it will snow or rain. If you are looking for a place to have a cabin built, the taiga biome is a beautiful and calm biome.
