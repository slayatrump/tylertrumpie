What are Biomes?


    Biomes are certain areas around the world that have very different animals, plants, and weather patters than eachother. Biomes can range from the oh so very cold polar biome all the way to the vacation worthy tropical biome. Each biome has its own personal characteristics that set itself apart from the rest of the biomes, making each and every biome super unique!

    This website will let you explore the wonders of seven different biomes and what they have to offer. Press on any of the biomes above to learn about that biome!
