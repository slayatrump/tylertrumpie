The Aquatic Biome


    The aquatic biome is home to many different types of fish and even mammals that live underwater. One example is this sea turtle. Unlike most turtles, these turtles have arms and legs that are especially good at swimming instead of walking on land!

    Animals that you can find could include dolphins, turtles, sharks, fish, and even tiny creatures called plankton!

    People visit aquatic biomes to fish and to scuba dive. Scuba diving is popular because a lot of people like to find old broken ships that are at the bottom of the ocean, explore coral reefs, explore the deep depths of the dark ocean, and even just to look at the many types of fish in the sea.
