The Arctic Biome


    The arctic biome is very very cold. Many of the animals here such as the arctic fox here have grown more fur to stay warm in this snowy wonderland.

    There are actually quite a few animals that live in the arctic even though it is so cold. Some that live here are polar bears, arctic foxes, seals, and penguins.

    Common weather patterns are blizzards, snowstorms, and very cold winds that blow the snow around everywhere. If you are going to visit here make sure you bring a jacket and some hot chocolate!
