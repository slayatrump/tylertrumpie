The Tundra Biome


    The tundra biome is similar to the arid biome in the fact that they are both full of empty space and have a smaller amount of water than other biomes, but the tundra biome is a lot colder.

    Animals that live here include penguins, moose, reindeer, and swans.

    Weather is quite cold here, but not as cold as the arctic biome. Just like the arid biome, there is a lack of rain, but there is mostly snow throughout the year.
